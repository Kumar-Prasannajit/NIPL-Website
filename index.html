Navgyan Innovations Pvt. Ltd. acts as the parent

body and innovation studio behind a suite of

futuristic startup projects. Each initiative is built

to address critical needs in healthcare,

environment, agriculture, and digital

transformation.

WE BUILD SOLUTIONS THAT MATTER

MedTech & Healthcare

Developing human-first innovations that enhance diagnostics, accessibility, and healthcare delivery using advanced technologies like AI, ML, and smart sensing.

AgriTech & AquaTech

Creating tech-enabled systems that support sustainable farming and aquaculture, disease prevention, and productivity improvement through data-driven insights and IoT.

Digital Transformation

Delivering scalable digital platforms and tools — from automation to analytics — that empower individuals, startups, and enterprises in an increasingly digital-first economy.

AI, ML & IoT Integration

We embed cutting-edge intelligence into real-world applications, enabling smarter decisions, predictive capabilities, and seamless user experiences across sectors.

Research & Prototyping

Innovation starts in the lab. We explore, experiment, and iterate on ideas to build proof-of-concept solutions that pave the way for future-ready technologies.

Everything we do is driven by a single mission: to use technology for real-world impact. Whether it's health, environment, or society — we design solutions that create positive change.
